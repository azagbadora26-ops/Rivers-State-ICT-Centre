About Us

The Rivers State ICT Centre represents a landmark initiative designed to anchor the region's digital infrastructure. The facility was constructed and equipped through a corporate partnership with Zenith Bank Plc, serving as a major technological asset for the state. The tech hub was officially commissioned in May 2010 by the Executive Governor of Rivers State, His Excellency Chibuike Rotimi Amaechi, following the early foundational planning structures established by the administration in 2008 to drive public automation, technical capacity building, and youth empowerment. Operating as an independent state asset, the centre is tasked with transforming theoretical concepts into practical skills by supporting 9 specialized training paths: Data Analytics, Cyber Security, Web Application Development, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics, Extended Reality (XR), Cloud Computing, and Graphics Design.

Our Vision

To become a leading ICT training center in Nigeria.

Our Mission

To equip individuals with practical digital and technical skills.
